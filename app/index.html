What is it?

Authentic Weather is a simple weather app, intended to condense all the weather information you need into a few screens. You have no need to decypher synaptic charts, detailed condition reports or lengthy forecasts. You need to know what it’s like right now, and what it’s going to be later on.
Will it be raining? Sun shining? A blizzard signaling the beginning of a new ice age? It will take you a few seconds.

How do I get it?

To use Authentic Weather, simply open it in your mobile browser, no installation required. If you have an iDevice, save it to your homescreen for easy access and a cleaner look.
